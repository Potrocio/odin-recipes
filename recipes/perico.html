Perico

Description

Perico is a traditional dish in a few South American countries.

Perico is a simple egg recipe packed with incredible flavor.

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 1 tomato
  • 1/4 onion
  • Garlic seasoning
  • Chicken Buillon
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Cilantro
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Dice tomato and onion
  2. Crack and mix 4 eggs in a bowl add salt, pepper, and garlic seasoning
  3. Add 1 tbsp of butter to pan and set on stove on medium heat
  4. Add diced tomato and onion to pan and season with chicken buillon
  5. After onions are browned add eggs and mix until cooked
  6. Chop cilantro and add to finished eggs
  7. Serve and enjoy!
