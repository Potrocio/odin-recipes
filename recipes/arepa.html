Arepa

Description

An arepa is made out of corn flour dough and it is cooked and filled with many different ingredients.

Arepas are very well known in a both Colombia and Venezuela.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of P.A.N. flour (Makes two arepas)
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1/2 Teaspoon of Salt
  • Cheese
  • Lunch Meat
  • 2 Tbsp Butter

Steps

  1. Place flour, water, and salt in a bowl and mix.
  2. Fashion dough into 3/4 inch thick discs. Note: Each arepa should be as wide as your palm.
  3. Add 1 tbsp of butter into a frying pan and set it on the stove on medium heat.
  4. Place dough discs on the pan and cover with lid.
  5. Cook each side for 6 minutes. Optional: You can leave arepas on each side until browned.
  6. Take a butter knife and carve a pocket into each arepa to put the filling in.
  7. Place 1/2 tbsp of butter into each arepa.
  8. Place Cheese and Lunch meat inside of each arepa
  9. Enjoy!
