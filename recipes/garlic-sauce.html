Garlic Sauce

Description

This Garlic sauce is simple and can easily enhance any meal

Enjoyed often with fingerfoods!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup Mayo
  • 4 Garlic cloves
  • 1/4 teaspoon of Salt
  • 1/4 cup of chopped cilantro

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients in a blender and turn on blender. Tip: If ingredients are not mixing you can add a small amount of water
  2. Enjoy with your favorite fingerfood!
